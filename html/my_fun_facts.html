The Cheat Sheet by Sarah Adams

The reason I love this book is because it's a cute romance novel. It's about these two best friends, Bree and Nathan, who secretly have feelings for each other but are too scared to say anything incase it ruins their friendship. I loved how they ended up together in the end and Bree's character was my favorite because she was so lovable and funny.
